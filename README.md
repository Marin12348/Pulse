# Pulse
A repository for Pulse Markets App
Privacy Policy

This application ("Pulse") is designed to provide financial market data for informational purposes only.

Data Collection
Pulse does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. No account creation is required, and no personally identifiable information is processed.

Usage Data
The application may use standard network requests to retrieve publicly available financial data. No user-specific tracking is performed.

Third-Party Services
Market data displayed in the app is sourced from publicly available financial data providers through third-party APIs. These providers may have their own privacy policies and terms of service.

Data Sharing
Pulse does not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

Security
Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of personal data exposure through this application.

Changes
This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected within the application or on the associated listing.
